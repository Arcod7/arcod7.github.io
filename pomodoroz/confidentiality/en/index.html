Pomodoroz Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Pomodoroz collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Pomodoroz mobile application.

Information We Collect

Pomodoroz does not collect any personal data or personally identifiable information from its users. The App operates in a completely local environment, and all data related to the Pomodoro technique is stored locally on the user's device.

How We Use Collected Information

Since Pomodoroz does not collect any user information, we do not use any collected information for any purpose. The App is designed to respect user privacy by keeping all data local and not transmitting it to any external servers.

Sharing Your Personal Information

Pomodoroz does not share any personal information or data with third parties, as no such information is collected in the first place.
